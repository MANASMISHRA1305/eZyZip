Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 business days with additional charges.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped.

What if I'm not available for delivery?

Our delivery partner will attempt delivery 3 times. You can also reschedule delivery by contacting us.

Do you deliver to remote areas?

We deliver to most areas in India. For remote locations, delivery may take longer and additional charges may apply.
